# Code of Conduct

## Our Goal

Intent-Aware UI is an open research initiative exploring new interaction paradigms for human-computer interaction.

We welcome designers, developers, researchers, accessibility specialists and anyone interested in improving digital interactions.

Our goal is to encourage thoughtful discussion, constructive criticism and evidence-based contributions.

---

## Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and professional.
- Assume good intentions.
- Welcome different perspectives.
- Base arguments on reasoning, experimentation or research whenever possible.
- Help improve ideas rather than defend opinions.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Personal attacks or harassment.
- Discrimination of any kind.
- Trolling or intentionally disruptive behavior.
- Bad-faith discussions.
- Dismissive or disrespectful communication.

---

## Design Discussions

Intent-Aware UI is an exploratory project.

Disagreement is expected.

Healthy debate is encouraged.

Ideas should be challenged.

People should not.

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove or reject contributions that do not follow this Code of Conduct.

Repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent exclusion from the project.

---

## Contact

For questions regarding this Code of Conduct, please open a GitHub Discussion or contact the project maintainers.
